About Movie:

       "Maharshi" is a Telugu-language film directed by Vamshi Paidipally, starring Mahesh Babu, Pooja Hegde, and Allari Naresh. The film revolves around a successful businessman who revisits his past to reconnect with his roots and help the people who were instrumental in his journey to success. It explores themes of friendship, social responsibility, and the impact of one's actions on society. The movie received positive reviews for its storyline, performances, and emotional depth.
